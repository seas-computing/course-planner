Enumeration ABSENCE_TYPE

Describes the different types of leave available to Faculty members at Harvard.

For more information regarding the types of leave available - see the FAS website

SABBATICAL_ELIGIBLE: = "SABBATICAL_ELIGIBLE"
  • Defined in src/common/constants/absenceType.ts:22

Faculty member is not currently on sabbatical, but is eligible to take a sabbatical. Usually sabbatical is available only to tenured professors.
